What We Do

Each year, tens of thousands of elephants are poached, along with tons of other animals. At Simba's Surveillance, we are dedicated to protecting wildlife from the growing threat of poaching.

Using state-of-the-art technology, our advanced camera systems provide live footage from the African Savanna, allowing us to detect trespassing poachers in real time. The moment a trespasser is identified, park rangers are alerted, enabling rapid response to safeguard the region's species.
